[% DEFAULT title="Padre Plugin Swarm" description="" %] [% WRAPPER lib/xhtml_doc %] [% WRAPPER chrome/styled_panel %] Plugins = {{{Padre::Plugin::Swarm}}} Aim to provide a network transport layer and pluggable services that are transport agnostic. Transmit messages to other participants in the swarm. Including but not limited to
  • instant messaging
  • snippets , an analogue of nopaste et al
  • cursor radar - see where and who has a cursor in '$commonfile'
  • games and time wasters (an absolute MUST)
  • pieces of geometry, Graph's describing objects in the SwarmVerse
